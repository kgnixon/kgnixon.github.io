Conceptual Design / Fusion 360 CAD / DFM

ANIMATRONIC NIGHTJAR

Conceptual Design / SolidWorks CAD / Manufacturing / Simulation & Testing

For my final year project at Imperial College London, I redesigned and built an animatronic nightjar to aid in conservation efforts. I planned the project, developed and modelled my own wing-flapping mechanism, and manufactured a final prototype for testing. The work culminated in a final report, seminar, and public poster presentation.

MARS ROVER DRILL

Conceptual Design / Fast Prototyping / SolidWorks CAD / Manufacturing

In this multi-team project, my group needed to design a drill to extract Martian soil samples. Solutions were discovered through my skills in fast prototyping, CAD, and manufacturing. The result of this project was a fully operational sampling system with succesful connections to neighbouring systems.
